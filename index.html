About

I am a PhD candidate in Biochemistry department at the National Dairy Research Institute, Karnal, India. I am interested in data-driven and experimental approaches to explore molecular mechanisms in biological systems. My research interests broadly include genomics, transcriptomics, and proteomics, with a focus on uncovering disease mechanisms and therapeutic targets through modern deep learning tools supported by experimental validations.
